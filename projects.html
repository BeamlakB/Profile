Name recogniton

A model trained to recognize names in informal text, such as emails. To accomplish this, the Enron corpus data- which contains over 700 emails. This CRF model was able to have 90% percison and 71% F1 Score.

Grit Market

A website that aims to provide a secure online platform for UMBC students to sell items. By offering a central hub for item listings, GritMarketplace empowers students to easily find and connect with potential buyers or sellers within the UMBC community.

Disease Predicton

Developed a model using patient-reported symptoms to predict potential diseases. We utilized LLAMA for symptom extraction through prompt engineering. Then used Information Retrieval, to identify correlations between symptom combinations and diseases.

Breast Cancer Diagnostic

A model detecting breast cancer, focusing on accurately distinguishing between benign and malignant cases, in order to minimize misdiagnosis. Implented classification algorithms such as logistic regression, support vector machines (SVM), decision trees, and random forests.
